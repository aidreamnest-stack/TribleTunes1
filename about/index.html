About TribleTunes

Empowering Emerging Artists & Music Lovers

TribleTunes helps emerging artists get the recognition they deserve. We support talented creators worldwide and provide a platform for their music to reach a wider audience.

On YouTube, we produce high-quality lyric videos, bringing music to life visually. Our goal is to expand reach, connect artists with fans, and make every song an experience to remember.

Whether you are an artist seeking exposure or a music lover exploring new talent, TribleTunes is your gateway to discovering amazing music.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Global Reach

Founded in February 2024, TribleTunes empowers emerging artists. In just over a year, we've reached 3M+ views and produced 400+ lyric videos, building a vibrant music community.

We continue to grow, connecting talented creators with fans, offering a space where music is celebrated worldwide.

Our Mission & Vision

Creating a Community That Celebrates Music

Our mission: help artists shine and make music discovery enjoyable. Every artist deserves recognition, every listener a memorable experience.

TribleTunes aims to expand globally, supporting artists, introducing new features, and fostering a community where music is felt, not just heard.
