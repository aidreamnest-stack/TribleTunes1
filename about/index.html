About TribleTunes

Empowering Emerging Artists & Music Lovers

TribleTunes is dedicated to helping emerging artists get the recognition they deserve. We are building a label to support talented artists worldwide and provide a platform for their music to reach a wider audience.

On YouTube, we create high-quality lyric videos, bringing music to life with visual storytelling. Our goal is to expand your reach, connect artists with fans, and make every song an experience to remember.

Whether you are an artist seeking exposure or a music lover exploring new talent, TribleTunes is your gateway to discovering amazing music and being part of a growing community.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Global Reach

TribleTunes was founded in February 2024 with the mission to empower emerging artists. In just over a year, we've reached over 3 million views and produced 400+ lyric videos, building a vibrant community of music lovers worldwide.

Our platform continues to grow as we connect talented creators with fans, offering a space where music is celebrated and shared across the globe.

Our Mission & Vision

Creating a Community That Celebrates Music

Our mission is simple: help talented artists shine and make music discovery enjoyable for everyone. We believe that every artist deserves recognition, and every listener deserves a memorable experience.

Looking forward, TribleTunes aims to expand its platform, supporting artists globally, introducing new features for creators, and fostering a community where music is not just heard but felt.
